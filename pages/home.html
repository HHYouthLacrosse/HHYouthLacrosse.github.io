Building a love of lacrosse one child, one stick, one experience at a time.
The Horseheads Youth Lacrosse Club is an organization which provides an opportunity for boys and girls in grades K-6 to play in a competitive spring field lacrosse league.

The Club is in its tenth year of existence and is a member of the Finger Lakes Lacrosse League. Club teams travel and play games throughout the Finger Lakes region including Elmira, Bath, Geneva, Waterloo, Midlakes, Seneca Falls, Penn Yan, Newark, and Palmyra-Macedonia. We have also established relationships with other leagues throughout western (Rochester) and Central NY (Cortland/Ithaca).
